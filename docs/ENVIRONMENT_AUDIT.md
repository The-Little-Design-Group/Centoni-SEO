```
    Ad Groups:
    - Investment Properties
    - Flip-Ready Renovations

Campaign: Partnerships
    Ad Groups:
    - Contractor Partnerships
    - Trade Partners
```

### Match Type Strategy

All keywords should be deployed in three match types:

1. **Exact Match:** [keyword phrase here]
   - Highest precision targeting
   - Best for high-intent, high-value terms

2. **Phrase Match:** "keyword phrase here"
   - Balanced reach and relevance
   - Captures variations with core intent

3. **Broad Match Modified:** +keyword +phrase +here
   - Maximum reach with keyword anchors
   - Requires strong negative keyword list

## Negative Keyword Strategy

Critical for protecting ad spend and maintaining lead quality:

### Exclude Categories:
- DIY and self-help searches (diy, how to, tutorial)
- Budget/discount seekers (cheap, affordable, budget, discount)
- Job seekers (jobs, careers, hiring, employment, internship)
- Educational searches (classes, courses, certification, school)
- Supply searches (supplies, tools, materials, equipment)
- Irrelevant platforms (craigslist, yelp reviews, bbb complaints)
